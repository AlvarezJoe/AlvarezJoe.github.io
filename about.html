About Me

Turning Data Into Intelligence, One Model At A Time

Hello, I'm Joemichael

I'm a Senior Data Scientist at Lennar, where I build production-ready AI systems that deliver measurable business impact. Based in Miami, Florida, I specialize in MLOps, machine learning, and scalable data solutions that solve complex enterprise challenges.

Currently pursuing my Master's in Analytics at Georgia Tech, I hold a Bachelor's in Data Analytics from Miami Dade College. My experience spans from financial analytics at Boeing Distribution Services to leading AI initiatives at Lennar, where I focus on enterprise-scale ML systems and production deployments.

What drives me is creating intelligent systems that streamline operations and deliver real business value. From developing enterprise-scale AI customer service agents to implementing MLOps infrastructure with drift monitoring and automated retraining pipelines, I focus on building reliable, scalable solutions.

My recent work includes developing AI-powered chatbot systems currently in MVP stage, along with building unstructured data pipelines that transform PDFs into actionable insights using LLMs and OCR technology. I specialize in taking experimental models from concept to production-ready systems.

When I'm not building models or optimizing pipelines, you'll find me exploring the latest developments in AI research, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring data scientists.
