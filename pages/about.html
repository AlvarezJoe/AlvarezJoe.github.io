About Me

Turning Data Into Intelligence, One Model At A Time

Hello, I'm Joemichael

I'm an AI Development Lead / Manager at The Carlyle Group (Carlyle AlpInvest Technology), where I lead Palantir Foundry as a core software and AI platform for workflow orchestration across AlpInvest teams. Based in Miami, Florida, I specialize in MLOps, machine learning, and scalable LLM-powered solutions for enterprise challenges.

I hold a Bachelor's in Data Analytics from Miami Dade College. My experience spans from financial analytics at Boeing Distribution Services to AI platform leadership at Carlyle, with a focus on enterprise-scale ML systems, production deployment, and cross-functional delivery.

What drives me is creating intelligent systems that streamline operations and deliver measurable business value. From launching customer-facing AI support experiences to building MLOps infrastructure with drift monitoring, automated alerting, and retraining, I focus on reliable and practical solutions.

Recent work includes designing enterprise GenAI support agents, building unstructured data pipelines that transform large PDF corpora into actionable insights using LLMs and OCR, and operationalizing ML workflows from concept to production.

When I'm not building models or optimizing pipelines, you'll find me exploring the latest developments in AI research, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring data scientists.
